Brazil 's Eletropaulo names new president .
SAO PAULO 1996-08-27
Sao Paulo state power firm Eletropaulo said it has named Eduardo Bernini as new president , replacing Emmanuel Sobral , who will head a secretariat at the Transportation Ministry .
Bernini is expected to take office Thursday , a Eletropaulo spokeswoman said .
