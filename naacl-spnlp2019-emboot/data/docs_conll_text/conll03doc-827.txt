SOCCER - THREE PULL OUT OF DUTCH SQUAD FOR BRAZIL .
AMSTERDAM 1996-08-29
Three Dutch players have pulled out of the squad for Saturday 's friendly international soccer match against Brazil in Amsterdam .
Ajax defender John Veldman and his team mate Richard Witschge are injured , while PSV midfielder Philip Cocu has a fever .
Dutch coach Guus Hiddink called in Feyenoord midfielder Giovanni van Bronckhorst and Vitesse defender Ferdy Vierklau for Cocu and Veldman , but did not name a replacement for midfielder Witschge .
