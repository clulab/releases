SOCCER - GOTHENBURG PUT FERENCVAROS OUT OF EURO CUP .
BUDAPEST 1996-08-21
IFK Gothenburg of Sweden drew 1-1 ( 1-0 ) with Ferencvaros of Hungary in the second leg of their European Champions Cup preliminary round tie played on Wednesday .
Gothenburg go through 4-1 on aggregate .
