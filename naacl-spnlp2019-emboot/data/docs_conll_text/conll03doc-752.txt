AEI 's Spanish operation wins ISO 9002 .
LONDON 1996-08-28
Air Express International said in a statement that Spain has become the twenty-second country in its network to achieve ISO 9002 quality accreditation .
It added Bureau Veritas has accredited AEI Iberfreight 's offices at Alicante , Barcelona , Bilbao , Madrid , Seville and Valencia as meeting the necessary standards .
