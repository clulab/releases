Czech CNB-120 index rises 1.2 pts to 869.3 .
PRAGUE 1996-08-22
The CNB-120 index , a broad daily measure of Czech equities , rose 1.2 points on Thursday to 869.3 , the Czech National Bank ( CNB ) said .
Eight of the ten sectoral indices rose , with the banking index rising the most , up 14.4 points to 1,294.5 .
-- Prague Newsroom , 42-2-2423-0003
