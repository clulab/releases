Alpha Techs closes Lockhart purchase .
NEW YORK 1996-08-23
Alpha Technologies Group Inc said it had closed on its agreement to acquire Lockhart Industries Inc .
The company said in a statement late on Thursday that it issued 280,556 shares of common stock for the stock of Lockhart .
These shares are subject to post-closing adjustments .
Lockhart , based in Paramount , Calif. , is a designer and manufacturer of sophisticated thermal management products .
