DUTCH CAPTAIN BLIND ENDS INTERNATIONAL CAREER .
AMSTERDAM 1996-08-25
Dutch soccer captain Danny Blind has decided to end his international career , Ajax spokesman David Endt said on Sunday .
Endt told Dutch news agency ANP that Blind , 35 , would no longer be available for selection for the national squad .
The Ajax defender , who led the Netherlands into the quarter-finals at June 's European championship finals in England , had decided to devote his attention to playing for his Amsterdam club , Endt said .
Blind , who played in the 1990 World Cup and the 1992 European championship , was capped 42 times for the Netherlands .
